HP G5EE

Introduction

The HP G5EE is the most used chromebook in the district at the current moment, we have hundreds of them in use. They have few issues however, which is welcome. Their strongest quality is likely their durability.

Common Issues

The G5EE has only one common problem:

On occassion, the connector for the battery will come loose, and won't turn on or charge.

The problem is, thankfully, very easy to fix. All you have to do is remove the keyboard, unplug the battery, and plug it back in.

Bezel Removal

The bezel is removed much like the G5. Two small screws at the bottom underneath a sticker, and otherwise it just snaps off with plastic clips. Removal is a cinch.

Keyboard Removal

The keyboard is easy to remove - 10 screws release from the back panel, each underneath a small rubber cover, and the keyboard just snaps out, similar to the bezel.
